Who I Am

About Me

My Passion for Accounting

I see Accounting as more than working with numbers — it is about understanding the information behind every decision. Financial records reflect how organisations plan, manage resources, and create sustainable growth. I am drawn to how accounting transforms data into meaningful insights that support responsible decision-making.

My Character & Strengths

I am naturally organised and enjoy analysing information, identifying patterns, and understanding how different factors influence outcomes. Seven years of Chinese Dance and student leadership have shaped me into a disciplined, meticulous individual with a strong sense of accountability and integrity.

Accounting represents the connection between logical thinking, responsibility, and real-world impact. I hope to continue developing the discipline, integrity, and skills needed to grow into a trusted accounting professional.

— Teo Yi Rou

Why Ngee Ann Polytechnic

Why NP Accountancy

Strong Foundation + Practical Learning

I am particularly interested in NP's Accountancy course because it combines strong accounting foundations with real-world application. What attracts me most is understanding how financial information is prepared, analysed, and used to guide business decisions — not just recording transactions.

Collaborative Environment

NP's emphasis on hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills aligns with my learning style — I learn best through applying concepts, solving problems, and collaborating with others. I value NP's environment where students learn from different perspectives, which will help me grow the teamwork and communication skills essential in accounting.
